MY FAMILY

THE SOURCE OF STRENGTH AND MOTIVATIONS IN MY LIFE

Here's a time line of My Parent's life:

  • 1973--1975 - Born in Nueva Ecija - Pangasinan , Philippines
  • 1991-1992 - Graduated from high school and embarked on diverse career paths, each one unique and purposeful."
  • 1990 - My father began working on farms to meet his immediate needs and secure his future aspirations."
  • 2007 - My mother ventured abroad to work, earning money to ensure my sister's education was completed.
  • 1993 - In 1993, they came together in marriage, marking the beginning of their shared commitment to each other and their dreams. With unwavering dedication, they set out to lay a solid foundation for their future. This union not only brought two hearts together but also ignited their shared vision of building a loving and supportive family. Together, they embraced the challenges and joys of life, creating a legacy built on love, resilience, and unity."
  • 1995 - In the years that followed, their hard work and perseverance bore fruit as they afforded to build a house, turning bricks and mortar into the beginning of their dream home. With meticulous care and love, they transformed this structure into a warm and inviting sanctuary, where every corner echoed with memories and every room resonated with laughter and love. This house became more than just a physical dwelling; it became a symbol of their shared aspirations, a testament to their determination, and the heart of their growing family.
  • 1996 - In the midst of their journey, a new chapter unfolded with the birth of my sister. Welcoming her into the world filled their lives with an indescribable joy and a profound sense of purpose. With her arrival, their family became complete, adding another layer of love, laughter, and shared dreams to their growing tapestry of life. She became the light of their lives, inspiring them to strive harder, dream bigger, and cherish every moment together as they continued to build their home and nurture their family bonds.
  • 2000 - As life continued to unfold, each passing day brought new challenges and opportunities, shaping their family's story with moments of resilience, growth, and shared experiences. Together, they navigated the ebb and flow of life's journey, drawing strength from their unity and the love that bound them. Through every triumph and setback, they remained steadfast in their commitment to each other and their family, laying the groundwork for a future filled with hope, understanding, and unwavering support.
  • 2004 - In 2004, a new and cherished chapter began with my birth, adding another dimension to our family's tapestry. My arrival brought boundless joy and a renewed sense of wonder into their lives, transforming our home into a lively haven filled with laughter, learning, and unconditional love. As I grew, I became a living testament to their shared values and aspirations, a reminder of the legacy they were building and the dreams they were nurturing for the future. With each passing day, we continued to weave our individual stories into the rich and vibrant fabric of our family's collective journey.
  • 2017 - In 2017, another milestone marked our family's journey as my sister proudly graduated from college. Her graduation was a momentous occasion, a testament to her dedication, hard work, and the unwavering support of our family. As she walked across the stage, diploma in hand, we celebrated not just her academic achievements but also the values of perseverance and determination that she embodied. Her graduation became a source of inspiration for all of us, reinforcing the belief that with passion and commitment, dreams can indeed be turned into reality.
  • 2022 - n 2022, I embarked on my own academic journey by starting college, following in the footsteps of my sister and carrying forward the family's commitment to education and personal growth. As I stepped onto campus, filled with anticipation and excitement, I felt a profound sense of gratitude for the foundation laid by my parents and the opportunities that awaited me. This new chapter in my life symbolized not just a continuation of my education but also a rite of passage, a step towards independence, and a chance to explore my passions and aspirations. With the unwavering support of my family behind me, I was eager to embrace the challenges and opportunities that college life had to offer, confident in the knowledge that I was building upon the legacy of love, resilience, and determination that defined our family.
  • 2023 - In 2023, my mother once again made the courageous decision to work abroad, driven by her unwavering commitment to support and fulfill my needs as I continued my college education. Her sacrifice and selflessness were a testament to the boundless love and dedication she had for our family. Despite the distance that separated us, her presence was always felt through her constant encouragement, guidance, and the unwavering belief she had in my abilities. Her efforts served as a poignant reminder of the sacrifices made for the sake of our dreams and aspirations, reinforcing the values of resilience, determination, and familial support that have always been the cornerstone of our family's journey.
  • 2024 - Despite the distances and challenges we faced, we remained a close-knit and happy family, bound together by love, understanding, and shared values. Our small yet resilient family unit became a source of strength and inspiration for each of us, reminding us of the importance of cherishing the moments we shared and supporting one another through thick and thin. Whether it was celebrating milestones, overcoming obstacles, or simply enjoying quality time together, we found joy and fulfillment in each other's company. Our family's story was not defined by the size of our household but by the depth of our connections, the resilience of our spirit, and the unwavering love that continued to unite us, no matter the distance or circumstances.
  • 2026 - In 2026, I held onto the hope and determination to graduate on time, fueled by my aspirations and dreams for the future. As the culmination of years of hard work, dedication, and the unwavering support of my family, graduation represented more than just an academic achievement; it symbolized a stepping stone towards realizing my dreams and carving out my path in the world. With the lessons learned, experiences gained, and the values instilled in me by my family, I was poised to embark on the next chapter of my journey with confidence and optimism. I remained steadfast in my commitment to pursuing my dreams, knowing that with perseverance and resilience, I could make them a reality and continue to build upon the legacy of determination, love, and unity that defined our small yet mighty family.
  • FUTURE.1 - Looking towards the future, I am driven by a deep-seated desire to fulfill the dreams that my parents were unable to pursue due to the constraints of poverty. Their sacrifices and unwavering commitment to our family have always served as a powerful source of inspiration for me, fueling my determination to break free from the limitations that held them back and to create opportunities for myself and future generations. With the education, values, and resilience instilled in me by my family, I aspire to turn their unrealized dreams into reality, honoring their legacy and ensuring that their sacrifices were not in vain. By pursuing my passions with dedication and purpose, I hope to pave the way for a brighter and more prosperous future, not just for myself but for our family as a whole, continuing the cycle of hope, ambition, and success that has defined our journey thus far.
  • FUTURE.2 - Above all else, my ultimate goal is to stay happy and healthy, cherishing each moment and prioritizing my well-being both mentally and physically. In a world filled with challenges and uncertainties, I recognize the importance of nurturing my mind, body, and spirit, as they are the foundation upon which I can build a fulfilling and meaningful life. By maintaining a balance between my aspirations, responsibilities, and personal needs, I aim to create a life that is not only successful but also sustainable and joyful. With the love and support of my family guiding me, I am committed to embracing a lifestyle that promotes happiness, health, and overall well-being, ensuring that I can continue to pursue my dreams and make a positive impact on the world around me.

Life itself is an achievement, a journey filled with moments of growth, learning, and self-discovery. Each day presents us with opportunities to overcome challenges, embrace new experiences, and forge meaningful connections with others. Whether it's celebrating small victories or reaching major milestones, every step we take contributes to our personal and collective growth, shaping our character and defining our path in life. By recognizing and appreciating the inherent value and beauty of each moment, we can cultivate a deeper sense of gratitude, purpose, and fulfillment. Ultimately, life's truest achievement lies not in the accolades we accumulate or the goals we attain, but in the love we share, the lives we touch, and the legacy of resilience, compassion, and joy we leave behind for future generations.

-- Glenn Abragon
